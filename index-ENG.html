Overview

Barra Beach House is located in Fortim, a coastal town in the state of Ceará, Brazil. The hotel offers comfortable rooms, a beautiful swimming pool, and a lush garden. From the hotel, guests can enjoy stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean and the famous Barra Beach.

Amenities

  • Comfortable rooms
  • Beautiful swimming pool
  • Lush garden
  • Stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean and Barra Beach

Location

Barra Beach House is located in Fortim, Ceará, Brazil. The hotel is situated just a few steps from Barra Beach, one of the most famous beaches in the state.
